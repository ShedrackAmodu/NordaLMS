GDPR Compliant

NexusLMS is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements for data protection and privacy.

1. Our GDPR Commitment

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a comprehensive data protection law that came into effect on May 25, 2018. NexusLMS is committed to GDPR compliance across our services.

2. Data Protection Principles

We adhere to the core principles of GDPR:

  • Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency
  • Purpose Limitation
  • Data Minimization
  • Accuracy
  • Storage Limitation
  • Integrity and Confidentiality

3. Your Rights Under GDPR

The GDPR provides you with several rights:

  • Right to Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Erasure
  • Right to Restrict Processing
  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to Object
